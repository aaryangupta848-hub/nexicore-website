Overview

The Goods and Services Tax (GST) regime requires precise and timely compliance. Nexicore offers end-to-end GST solutions to help your business stay compliant and avoid penalties.

Regular Filing

We manage your monthly and quarterly filings with accuracy:

  • GSTR-1: Filing details of outward supplies.
  • GSTR-3B: Monthly summary return filing.
  • GSTR-2A / 2B Reconciliation: Matching input tax credit with vendor data.
  • Nil Returns: Filing for periods with no activity.

Registration & Setup

Starting a new venture? We help you get set up:

  • New GST Registration for individuals and companies.
  • Amendments to core and non-core fields in registration.
  • Cancellation & Revocation of GST registration.
  • LUT Filing for exporters.
  • DSC (Digital Signature Certificate) Setup.

Annual & Advisory

Long-term compliance and support:

  • GSTR-9 / 9C: Annual returns and reconciliation statements.
  • GST Audit Support: Assisting in departmental audits.
  • Refunds & Notices: Handling refund applications and responding to notices.
  • Compliance Health Check: Reviewing your GST practices for gaps.
