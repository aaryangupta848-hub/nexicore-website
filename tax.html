Overview

Navigating the complex landscape of direct taxation requires expert guidance. At Nexicore, we provide comprehensive solutions for individuals, businesses, and corporates to ensure compliance and optimize tax liabilities.

TDS Services

We handle all aspects of Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) compliance:

  • Salary TDS: Computation and filing for employees.
  • Vendor / Contractor TDS: Compliance for payments to contractors and professionals.
  • NRI TDS: Specialised services for non-resident payments.
  • Property Purchase TDS: Form 26QB filing assistance.
  • TDS Corrections: Rectification of defaults and errors.
  • Lower Deduction Certificate: Assistance in obtaining certificates for lower TDS.

TCS Services

Our Tax Collected at Source (TCS) services include:

  • Goods Sale TCS compliance.
  • Foreign Remittance (LRS) TCS.
  • E-commerce operator TCS filings.
  • Issuance of TCS Certificates.

ITR Filing

We simplify Income Tax Return filing for various categories:

  • Salaried Individuals: Accurate filing to maximize refunds.
  • Capital Gains: Calculation for stock market and property transactions.
  • Business Owners: Presumptive and regular business taxation.
  • Companies & Trusts: Corporate tax returns and trust compliance.
  • Updated Returns: Filing ITR-U for missed or corrected returns.

Tax Advisory

Beyond filing, we offer strategic support:

  • Tax Audits under Section 44AB.
  • Handling Income Tax Notices and scrutiny assessments.
  • Income Tax Refund assistance.
  • PAN & TAN application and correction services.
